Popular Destinations

Kutch

India's largest seasonal saline wetland sanctuary in Gujarat's Kutch district.

Kerela

Kerala's backwater villages offer glimpses into a unique water-bound life, best experienced by cruising through the canals and lakes of popular destinations like Alappuzha (Alleppey), Kumarakom, Kollam, and the Kuttanad region.

Coorg

Coorg is famous for its lush natural beauty, extensive coffee and spice plantations, mist-covered hills, and unique Kodava culture. Often called the "Scotland of India".

The fern Royal Farm Resort

Kumarakom Lake Resort

Kumarakom Lake Resort, acclaimed as the finest luxury heritage resort in India, nestles on the serene banks of the Lake Vembanad, the vast stretch of tranquil, emerald green backwaters, in one of India's popular holiday spots, Kerala.

Coorg Wilderness Resort

Come, take a step into wilderness and discover a rare ethos of luxury embedded in the gracious lap of nature. Nestled amid the deep valleys and majestic hills of Coorg
